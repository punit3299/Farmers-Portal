Our History

Started in 2019, Farmer's Portal quickly established itself as a major helping tool to the farmers of The Agricultural Land INDIA. We started this website in order to provide a good technical help and a good grip over the market so that the farmers know their product's value in the market without being cheated.

Our motive is to never letting a farmer suffer from bad harvest and he gets his rightful share from the market for which he devotes his months of life and be happy.
